About Parola

Parola is a town in the Jalgaon district of Maharashtra, known for its rich history and culture. It is famous for the Parola Fort and has various temples that reflect the architectural beauty of the region.

Interesting Facts

Historical Fort

Parola Fort dates back to the 14th century.

Agriculture Hub

The town is known for its agriculture, particularly the cultivation of pulses and cotton.

Cultural Festivals

Parola hosts various cultural festivals that attract visitors from neighboring regions.
