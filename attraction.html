Attractions in Parola

Shree Balaji Temple

Parola is famous for Shree Balaji, who first arrived here 345 years ago. The annual procession for Shree Balaji attracts many visitors from across Maharashtra.

Parola Fort

A historical fort built by Khander Rao around the 16th century, it offers stunning architecture and panoramic views, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

Madkay Maroti Temple

This temple is well-known among locals and visitors alike, offering a serene place of worship and spiritual reflection.

Old Jain Temple

Home to the revered 16 No Bhagawan Shri Shantinath Bhagwan Murti, believed to be around 1500 years old, this temple is rich in history and spirituality.
