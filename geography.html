Geography of Parola

Parola is located at 20.88°N latitude and 75.12°E longitude. It has an average elevation of 261 metres (856 feet).

Demographics

As of the 2001 India census, Parola had a population of 34,800. Males constituted 52% of the population, while females made up 48%.

The average literacy rate in Parola is 69%, higher than the national average of 59.5%. Male literacy stands at 76%, and female literacy is at 61%.

In Parola, 13% of the population was under the age of 6.
